# Type module: single-product-review

## When this applies
`articleType` in the brief is `"single-product-review"`. There is exactly one product in the brief. This is a standalone review - opinionated, thorough, and personal.

## Target word count
- **Total**: 500-1000 words
- No per-product section minimum (single product fills the whole article)

## Required structure

0. **H1 heading** (MANDATORY - first line of article)
   - Format: `# [Product name] anmeldelse - [one-line verdict or hook]`
   - Example: `# Dreame X50 Ultra Kombi Sort anmeldelse - er det en robot, der holder hvad den lover?`
   - Must contain the product name and "anmeldelse" or "test"

1. **Introduction** (60-100 words)
   - FIRST mention of the product name MUST be an affiliate link
   - Name the product immediately (linked) and state your overall take
   - Example: "[Produkt](url) er ikke den billigste robotstøvsuger på markedet - men det er heller ikke den vi kalder frem når nogen spørger om 'noget der bare virker'."
   - Hook with the product's defining characteristic (price, rank, watcher count from brief - do NOT name the price-comparison platform, see universal rules)

2. **Hvad er det?** (80-150 words, H2)
   - Brief overview: category, price, key specs from brief
   - Who it is designed for - be specific (not "den travle husstand", name actual use cases)
   - Image placement at the start of this section

3. **Det vi kan lide** (150-200 words, H2: "Det vi kan lide")
   - Bullet list with 4-5 concrete pros (spec-backed, from brief)
   - Each bullet: one thing + why it matters in practice (no vague "god ydelse")
   - Short conversational paragraph after the list - no filler phrases

4. **Det vi ville ændre** (80-120 words, H2: "Det vi ville ændre")
   - Bullet list with 2-3 honest cons - REAL product limitations, not meta-comments about missing data
   - No sugarcoating. A review with no cons loses credibility.

5. **Hvem er den til?** (60-100 words, H2: "Hvem er den til?")
   - "Køb den hvis..." - specific reason with data
   - "Overvej [specific named alternative] hvis..." - NAME the alternative product explicitly, do not say "overvej alternativet"
   - Widget placement here

6. **Vores dom** (H2: "Vores dom", 60-100 words)
   - Clear recommendation with a shorthand verdict (e.g., "4 ud af 5" or a sharp closing sentence)
   - Affiliate link on product name
   - Active CTA at the end: "Se aktuel pris hos alle [N] forhandlere →" or "Tjek om prisen er faldet siden sidst - [N]+ holder allerede øje."
   - One sentence that captures the essence of the product

## Tone
First-person plural ("vi har testet", "vores oplevelse er"). Opinionated. Honest.
The reader is deciding whether to spend their money - treat it seriously.
Warmth is good; false enthusiasm kills trust.

## CRO focus
- Single strong CTA at the end - don't dilute with multiple links
- Widget in "Hvem er den til?" section (decision moment)
- Verdict affiliate link is the primary conversion point

## What to avoid
- Missing H1 - every article MUST start with a `#` heading
- Comparing multiple products at length (there is only one product here)
- Vague pros like "god ydelse", "markant nemmere" - every claim needs a spec or concrete reason
- "I dette udvalg", "i denne anmeldelse" as opening phrases
- Artificial hedging - take a position
- "Overvej alternativet" without naming the alternative - always name it explicitly
- Meta-cons like "begrænset specifikationsdata" - only real product limitations
- "markedet taler sit eget sprog" and other hollow clichés
- "Den travle husstand" as a use-case description - be specific
- AI hedge phrases: "Det er alligevel rimeligt at nævne", "man kan argumentere for"
- "ligeledes", "endvidere", "desuden" as sentence starters
- Passive CTA in verdict - always use an active click verb
