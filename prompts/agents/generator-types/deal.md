# Type module: deal

## When this applies
`articleType` in the brief is `"deal"`. The top product has a significant recent price drop (≥10%) and/or active PriceRunner watchers. This is a time-sensitive, urgency-driven article.

## Target word count
- **Total**: 400-700 words — SHORT and punchy. Get to the point fast.
- No per-product section minimum

## Required structure

1. **Introduction** (50-80 words)
   - Open with the deal fact: price, drop percentage, or watcher count
   - Example: "[Produkt] er netop faldet 18% i pris — og det mærker markedet. Over 400 mennesker holder allerede øje."
   - No fluff. Every sentence must earn its place.

2. **The deal section** (150-250 words, H2: "Hvad er tilbuddet?" or "Hvorfor nu?")
   - Price history context: where it was, where it is now
   - The watcher signal: why others are watching this product
   - What the reader gets at this price (key specs from brief, concisely)
   - Widget placement here

3. **Is it worth it?** (80-150 words, H2)
   - One honest paragraph: who should grab this deal and who should wait
   - Link to alternative if the deal product isn't right for everyone
   - Keep it short. Readers are here for the deal, not a lecture.

4. **Verdict** (H2: "Vores dom", 50-80 words)
   - Short and decisive. Time-boxing language: "Til denne pris er det svært at finde noget bedre"
   - Affiliate link on the product name
   - Optional urgency: "Prisen kan stige igen — tjek aktuel pris nu"

## Tone
Punchy. Conversational. Slightly urgent without being slimy. Like a friend texting "hey, this went on sale."
Short sentences dominate. One-sentence paragraphs are fine here.

## CRO focus
- Urgency is the #1 signal — use price drop data directly
- Affiliate link appears within the first 100 words
- Widget in the deal section (not only in the verdict)
- No analysis paralysis — maximum two options mentioned

## What to avoid
- Long philosophical intros
- Comparing 5 products (this is ONE deal focus)
- "Analytisk set", "objektivt set", "i dette udvalg"
- Softening the urgency with too many caveats
