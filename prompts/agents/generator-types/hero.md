# Type module: hero

## When this applies
`articleType` in the brief is `"hero"`. The top product dominates (2× the popularity of #2, or a strong price-watcher signal). The article is a deep endorsement of one standout product, supported by brief mention of alternatives.

## Target word count
- **Total**: 700-1100 words
- No per-product section minimum (one product gets the full treatment)

## Required structure

0. **H1 heading** (MANDATORY — first line of article)
   - Format: `# [Product name] — [one-line hook referencing watchers/rank]`
   - Example: `# Dreame X50 Ultra Kombi Sort — robotstøvsugeren 50+ danskere holder øje med`
   - Must contain the focus keyword (category name) in natural form
   - Do NOT mention PriceRunner in the H1 or anywhere in the body — see universal rules

1. **Introduction** (80-150 words)
   - FIRST sentence must contain an affiliate link on the product name
   - Lead with the product name (linked) and its defining signal (watchers, price drop, rank)
   - Example opening: "Over 600 mennesker overvåger aktivt prisen på [Produkt](url) — og det er der en god grund til."
   - Affiliate disclosure in the first paragraph
   - Hook the reader into why this particular product earned the spotlight

2. **Deep-dive section** (400-600 words, H2 with product name)
   - Tell the story of the product: what it does differently, who it is for
   - Use all spec data from the brief. Cite concrete numbers
   - Include at least one product image placement early in this section
   - Add a bullet list (3-5 items) of concrete strengths — backed by spec numbers
   - One honest paragraph acknowledging what it does NOT do well (must be specific, not vague)
   - Add a soft mid-article CTA line before the widget: "Se aktuel pris og tilgængelighed hos alle [N] forhandlere →"
   - Widget placement after the soft CTA

3. **Alternatives** (100-150 words, H2: "Alternativer at overveje")
   - Briefly mention ALL other brief products for readers who want options
   - Each gets one sentence + affiliate link + ONE decision shortcut ("Vælg denne hvis...")
   - No detailed comparison.

4. **Verdict** (H2: "Vores dom", 60-100 words)
   - Restate WHY this hero product earns the recommendation with a concrete reason
   - Active CTA — NOT "hold øje med den". Use: "Tjek aktuel pris hos alle [N] forhandlere →" or "Se om prisen er faldet siden sidst →"
   - Affiliate link on the product name in the verdict

## Tone
Authoritative and warm. Like a colleague who tried this exact product and is genuinely excited.
First-person plural ("vi har testet", "vores erfaring") is acceptable here.
Avoid comparative superlatives — let the data do the arguing.

## CRO focus
- Affiliate link on the product name in the intro
- Widget placement at end of deep-dive
- Verdict MUST have an affiliate link
- Urgency angle if price-drop or watcher signal: "Prisen er faldet X% og 600+ følger med"
- Include 1 external link to the manufacturer's product page (see universal rules)

## What to avoid
- Missing H1 — every article MUST start with a `#` heading
- Per-product bullet lists comparing all products (this is not a roundup) — one bullet list on the HERO product is fine
- "I dette udvalg" — this is a single-star article
- Fabricated specs not in the brief
- Passive CTA like "hold øje med den" or "kig nærmere på den" — always use an active click verb
- AI hedge phrases: "Det er alligevel rimeligt at nævne", "det er værd at nævne", "man kan argumentere for"
- "ligeledes", "endvidere", "desuden" as sentence starters
- Uniform sentence length — mix short punchy sentences (5-8 words) with longer flowing ones
- "konstrueret til" — use "bygget til" or rephrase
- "signalerer en reel interesse" — use "viser", "afslører", or "det siger noget"
